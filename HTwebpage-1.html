What is the history of HerbieTech?

Many individuals are still terrified of getting sick after the pandemic, which is why a large number of us are currently taking immune-boosting medications. However, since inflation is a problem in the Philippines this year, a lot of Filipinos are searching for ways to cut costs, particularly when it comes to locating alternative medications. For this reason, our group decided to start a company called Herbietech, which will assist our fellow Filipinos in learning more about herbal remedies that are unique to their backyard or surrounding area.
